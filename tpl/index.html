Welcome To Wisec Pte Ltd

About Us

Wisec started off as a hobby of its owner, who likes to collect rare vintages. We are a licensed liquor selling company in Singapore that specialises in vintage wines. Wisec is managed by a small team of experienced wine and spirit lovers, who are dedicated to searching for and procuring rare and premium wines from reputable distilleries/ collectors or wineries around the world. We bring them into the Singapore market and to enable the people here to enjoy what is usually unavailable locally. Our services extend beyond merely procuring and distributing, all our wines are stored and delivered at the optimal temperature ensuring the uncompromised highest possible quality.

Please contact us to get quotation / order.
